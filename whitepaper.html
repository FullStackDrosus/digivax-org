Digital Immunity at the Protocol Layer

Modern systems fail not because of bugs — but because trust is externalized. DigiVax embeds verification directly into protocol execution paths, eliminating reliance on policy, perimeter defenses, and human oversight.

What DigiVax Is

DigiVax is a protocol-level integrity system that enables systems to assert computational validity permanently — regardless of cryptographic shifts or adversarial evolution.

What DigiVax Is Not

  • Not encryption
  • Not blockchain
  • Not monitoring
  • Not compliance software

Threat Model Alignment

DigiVax is designed for environments where automation exceeds human oversight, AI-driven attacks adapt faster than policy, and cryptographic assumptions cannot be trusted long-term.
